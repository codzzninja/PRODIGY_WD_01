Our Menu

Brick Oven Pizza

Brick oven pizza is known for its crispy crust and distinctive smoky flavor, achieved by baking at high temperatures in a wood-fired oven. This traditional method ensures even cooking and enhances the pizza's overall taste and texture.

Greek Pizza

Greek pizza features a thick, chewy crust topped with robust ingredients like olive oil, oregano, feta cheese, olives, and often a blend of mozzarella and cheddar. It's distinct for its abundant toppings and Mediterranean-inspired flavors.

French Break Pizza

French bread pizza uses a halved loaf of French bread as the base, resulting in a crispy, sturdy crust. It's typically topped with classic pizza ingredients like marinara sauce, cheese, and various toppings, offering a quick and easy alternative to traditional pizza dough.
